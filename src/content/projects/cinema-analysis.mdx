# Analysis of French Cinema Landscape

Analysis of the evolution of the French cinema landscape in the face of Covid-19 (2018 baseline), the rise of streaming, and inflation, with data preparation in Python, analyses in R, and visualization in Power BI on 2,042 establishments.

## Key Insights

- **Market Dynamics**: Highlighting variations in attendance and sector recovery dynamics.
- **Covid-19 Impact**: 75% drop in entries in 2021 compared to 2018, showing the major impact of the health crisis.
- **Sector Recovery**: Triple rebound in attendance in 2022, marking a strong recovery post-Covid.
- **Industry Resilience**: Maintenance of activity for the majority of cinemas, proof of the sector's great resilience.
